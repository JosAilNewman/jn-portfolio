About

I discovered the world of web development in my first graduate job where – as digital content writer and creator – I helped launch and latterly manage an editorial website. The moment I tried my hand at some basic HTML and CSS in wordpress, I knew front-end development was the right path for me. So, alongside my full-time comms role at a FTSE 30 company, I took on a part-time software development course with _nology.

Chilling in the sun with a beer, a good book and my cat, Derek.
